About me

Hi, I currently live in Stockholm, Sweden. I am originally from Uzbekistan, and I lived in Dubai from 2010 to 2019. I have a nice family - wife and 2 daughters. I am fluent in English, Russian and Uzbek languages. Nyligen började jag också lära mig svenska.

IT programming is my hobby and passion since my childhood - since my parents bought me my first computer when I was 11 years old. That time I started to learn such programming languages like basic and pascal. I have also bachelor's degree in Economics.

About 10-15 years ago I have made several commercial projects on PHP/MySQL with usage of CMS Joomla/PHP-nuke. I have also learned Java Core. Since the last couple of years I started refreshing my knowledge with new web technologies. I learned HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and now I focus my learning on ReactJS and all tools and libraries related to React. I like to learn and improve my knowledge during most of my free time. And I can see that nowadays the continious learning is one of the main requirements in web development.
I am currently positioning myself as a Middle Web Developer. And I would like to grow up to senior level in upcoming 4-5 years.
